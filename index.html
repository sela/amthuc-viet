Amthuc Viet is a family run business in Wadhurst East Sussex. We offer a Vietnamese food for takeaway.

We offer authentic Vietnamese food for you to collect 7 days a week between 11:30 to 14:30 and 17:00 to 20:00

We will be closed on the following dates: 26/10/2020, 27/10/2020 and 1/11/2020.

We ask you to place an order a day before so we can offer you the freshest meal, but don't put off if you want to eat on the same day, just give us a call and we might be able to serve you.
