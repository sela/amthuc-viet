Amthuc Viet is a family-owned business located in Wadhurst, East Sussex. We provide a Vietnamese catering service, as well as food for takeaway and cooking workshops.

We offer authentic Vietnamese cuisine, which you can collect from us on Wednesday through Saturday from 17:00 to 20:00. To place an order, you can call us starting at 10:00, or alternatively, send us a text or WhatsApp message at any time of the day.

Additionally, we can accommodate special dietary needs or preferences upon request. Our cooking workshops are also available. Please feel free to contact us for more information.
