The story of Amthuc Viet

Our family is a new member of the beautiful Wadhurst community in East Sussex. I am Vietnamese, grew up in the South of Vietnam. When I moved to London four years ago, I missed so much my homeland, and all the flavours of Vietnamese food. I started to find out shops that sell Vietnamese stuff in London, cook all my favourite dishes for my family. I was very happy having smelling all familiar flavours, see all the authentic meals available in my home, and the most important is my husband really enjoy what I made. He loves Vietnamese food too.

I started to cook more and more different dishes for my family and friends, and feel so good when they enjoy what I made. Some of my friends suggested "If you like so much to make food, why don't you start a food business?"

With great motivation from my husband, and the cooking inspiration. We have registration for Vietnamese takeaway at out new home in Wadhurst. I would love to bring you the special flavour of every single dishes, which is careful prepared from fresh ingredient and be cooked with my passion.

Most of materials are from local suppliers. To offer you the freshest food, we suggest to make your order at least a day in advance.
